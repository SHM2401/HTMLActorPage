Vijay

Born Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar was born on June 22, 1974 in Madras, Tamil Nadu. Career Vijay is one of the most popular and highest-paid actors in India. He has acted in over 60 films, including some of the highest-grossing Tamil films of all time. Nickname Vijay's fans call him "Thalapathy," which means "commander" in Tamil. Awards Vijay has won several awards, including three Tamil Nadu State Film Awards, a Kalaimamani, and an honorary doctorate from MGR University.
